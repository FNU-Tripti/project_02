Indoor Plants and their benefits

Plants that you can grow in your Bedroom

To get a good night sleep, It is important that the bedroom has a nice atmosphere. This is why many experts suggest to add plants to our space. Plants helps to reduce the indoor pollutants. Some researchers have even theorize that plants can make you feel relax. There is one problem though, there are lots of plants. Picking one out will be quite a task. So here are some tips for the plants that you can grow in your bedroom to get the maximum benefits.

For Example, Here is the list of such plants

  1. Lady Palm Plant

    Your bedroom is often at risk of being infested with microorganisms and radicals. These radicals can cause breathing problems and other health issues if they manage to enter your body. This is where our entry of the list comes to rescue. Lady Palm Plants are one of the most well-known natural air purifiers. They are capable of cleansing the air of formaldehyde, ammonia, xylene and toluene. It is also a low maintenance plant that doesn't require much nurturing. Watering it once or twice a day should be enough.

  2. Spider Plant

    If Spider-Man is your friendly neighbourhood hero, then Spider-Plant is your friendly roommate. This will be especially true if you have any form of dust allergy. Why? Because most of the experts beleive that Spider Plants can remove more than 90% of toxins. Unlike the Lady Palm though Spider-Plants have to pampered a liitle more.
